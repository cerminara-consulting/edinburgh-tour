Edinburgh · Old Town

One-Day Walking Tour

Nine stops from Edinburgh Castle to Armchair Books — all within a 15-minute walk of each other. Start at the top of the Royal Mile and work downhill.

Your stay

Courtyard by Marriott Edinburgh

1–3 Baxter’s Place, Edinburgh EH1 3AF

To the Castle: ~17 min on foot — head west on Princes Street, turn up The Mound, the Castle esplanade is at the top.

Back to base after stop 9: ~18 min uphill — Grassmarket → George IV Bridge → North Bridge, then a short walk to Baxter’s Place.

Route at a glance

From the hotel (★) up to the Castle, then downhill through the Old Town to Armchair Books (9). Total walk: ~3.1 km.

  1. 9:13 AM From your hotel

    ★

    Courtyard by Marriott Edinburgh

    Your stay · 1–3 Baxter’s Place

    Leave by 9:13 to make the 9:30 Castle slot. Walk west on Princes Street, turn up The Mound — the Castle esplanade is at the top. ~17 min on foot.

  2. 9:30 AM Morning

    1

    Edinburgh Castle

    ★ 4.6 (107,703) · Castle

    Book the first timed slot — crowds double by 11. Budget 2 hours. Stay for the 1pm gun if you run long.

  3. 11:45 AM

    2

    Camera Obscura & World of Illusions

    ★ 4.6 (15,881) · Museum

    Two doors down from the castle esplanade. Do the rooftop and the Victorian camera tour; skip floors if short on time.

  4. 1:00 PM Afternoon

    3

    The Witchery

    ★ 4.3 (4,033) · Restaurant

    Set lunch is the affordable way in — two courses around £30. Book well ahead; note their strict cancellation fee.

  5. 2:30 PM

    4

    The Writers' Museum

    ★ 4.4 (2,173) · Museum

    Free, small, 30–45 min. The entrance in Lady Stair's Close is easy to walk past — watch for it.

  6. 3:15 PM

    5

    Victoria Street

    — · Route

    The curved 'Diagon Alley' street. Walk the upper terrace level too — better photos, fewer people.

  7. 3:45 PM

    6

    Greyfriars Kirkyard

    ★ 4.7 (2,083) · Cemetery

    Thomas Riddell's grave is along the back wall path near the Flodden Wall gate. Free, open 24 hours.

  8. 4:15 PM

    7

    National Museum of Scotland

    ★ 4.8 (58,488) · Tourist Attraction

    Free. You only get ~45 min before 5pm close — hit the Grand Gallery, Dolly the Sheep, and the rooftop terrace view. Come back another day for the full 3 hours if it grabs you.

  9. 5:15 PM Evening

    8

    The Vennel Viewpoint

    ★ 4.7 (1,831) · Tourist Attraction

    Best castle photo in the city, framed by the stone steps. Golden hour is the move.

  10. 5:30 PM

    9

    Armchair Books

    ★ 4.7 (1,467) · Bookshop

    Secondhand stacks to the ceiling, open until 6:30. Check the vintage shelves at the back. Dinner after in the Grassmarket, right below you.

  11. 7:00 PM Back to base

    ⌂

    Courtyard by Marriott Edinburgh

    Return · ~18 min uphill

    Grassmarket → up George IV Bridge → North Bridge → short walk to Baxter’s Place. Or grab a black cab from the Grassmarket rank and be back in 5.
