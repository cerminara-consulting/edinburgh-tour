Edinburgh · Two days on foot · ~4–5 mi per day

Castle, kirkyard, and the quiet side of town

Two walking loops that follow the city's actual geography: Old Town top-to-bottom on day one, then Dean Village, Stockbridge and Calton Hill on day two. Tap any stop to find it on the map.

Day 1 — Old Town Day 2 — Stockbridge & Calton Hill
Day 01 / Old Town

Castle to Kirkyard

Start at the top of the Royal Mile and work downhill. Every stop is within 15 minutes of the last.

Day 02 / North & East

Stockbridge, New Town & Calton Hill

The quieter, prettier loop. Beat the crowds to Dean Village, brunch in Stockbridge, end on Calton Hill for sunset.

Book these before you go

Edinburgh Castle — timed entry sells out; take the first slot of the day. edinburghcastle.scot/tickets

The Witchery — the set lunch (~£30, two courses) is the affordable way in; note the strict cancellation fee. thewitchery.com

The Chocolatarium — the 90-minute tour where you make your own bar. chocolatarium.co.uk

In July the sun sets after 9:30 PM — the evening is longer than the schedule suggests. · Optional add-on: Arthur's Seat at sunrise (2 hrs, real elevation).
